philosophy

I hold degrees in marketing and psychology, which fuels my interest in understanding human behaviour. I excel at solving problems through Human-Centered design methods while aligning with business goals.

My approach to UX/UI design is shaped by Steve Krug's book, "Don't Make Me Think." This book remains relevant today, and I appreciate its mindset.

I believe that great design results from teamwork, emphasising effective collaboration and the sharing of ideas.
